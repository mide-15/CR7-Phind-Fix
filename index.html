Cristiano Ronaldo

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH, OMRIH, OMRI, OMN, OMN (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered one of the best players in the world, Ronaldo has won numerous awards, including the FIFA World Player of the Year, UEFA European Footballer of the Year, and the UEFA Best Player in Europe.

History

Ronaldo began his professional career at Sporting CP, where he was spotted by Manchester United scouts. He joined Manchester United in 2003 and quickly became a key player, winning several titles and individual awards. In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid, where he won multiple La Liga titles and the UEFA Champions League.

Accomplishments

Ronaldo has won numerous titles throughout his career, including multiple UEFA Champions League titles, La Liga titles, and the FIFA Club World Cup. He has also been named FIFA World Player of the Year multiple times.
